Lasagna

Description

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
